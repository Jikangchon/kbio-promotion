What is Bibimbap?

"Bibimbap" is a popular traditional Korean dish, featuring fresh vegetables, various meats, a fried egg, and spicy gochujang sauce served over steamed rice. It's a flavorful and nutritious meal where all the ingredients are mixed together to create a delicious harmony of tastes and textures.

We are offering a modified version of Korea's traditional dish, Bibimbap, tailored to suit the American palate, in order to create another unique culinary experience.
